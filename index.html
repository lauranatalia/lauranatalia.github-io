About Me

I’m Laura Garcia, a 25 years old graphic designer originally from Bogota, Colombia but now living in Ottawa, Canada. I believe in good design. Design that makes people happy. Design that is simple, modern, eye-catching and that pushes things forward. I believe that nobody can understand graphic design without the passion of those who are dedicated to this profession. Passion for art is in my blood. Color, composition and harmony in general are the basis of my work.
