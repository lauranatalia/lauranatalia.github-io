ABOUT ME

I’m Laura Garcia, a 25 years old graphic designer originally from Bogota, Colombia but now living in Ottawa, Canada. I believe in good design. Design that makes people happy. Design that is simple, modern, eye-catching and that pushes things forward. I believe that nobody can understand graphic design without the passion of those who are dedicated to this profession. Passion for art is in my blood. Color, composition and harmony in general are the basis of my work. My role is to create an appropriate combination of design and usability for an effective design. My work focuses on helping you communicate your message or idea. I love creating beautiful, simple and effective designs.

iPark App

iPark is an iOS app that allows people to pay parking using their phone. Simply enter a credit card number and add as many license plates as you wish, select how long you would like to park for and the cost will be automatically charged to the credit card. You can also set alerts to let you know when your session is about to expire.

iMap App

iMap is your personal guide when you visit a mall. iMap is an iOs app that works just like a GPS, except it is a navigation system that works indoors. This app helps you get around shopping malls, and find a store or any common area within a facility, it also helps you get there by showing you exactly which direction to go.
